Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Excaliframe handles your data.

The Short Version

Excaliframe does not collect any data. All your diagrams and data stay within your Confluence instance, managed by Atlassian.

Data Storage

Excaliframe stores all diagram data directly within Confluence using Atlassian's Forge platform. This means:

  • Your diagrams are stored in Confluence, not on external servers
  • Data is subject to Atlassian's security and privacy policies
  • You maintain full control over your data through Confluence

No External Data Collection

Excaliframe does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or telemetry
  • Store any user data outside of Confluence

Open Source

Excaliframe is open source software. You can review the complete source code at {{ .GitHubURL }} to verify our privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

Excaliframe runs on Atlassian's Forge platform. For information about how Atlassian handles data, please refer to Atlassian's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.

This policy is effective as of January 2025.
